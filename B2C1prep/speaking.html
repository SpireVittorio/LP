Speaking Part 4 A

Use Vocaroo https://vocaroo.com/ to record an audio in which you answer the following questions (3 minutes).

People sometimes say that teenagers are becoming too lazy. Do you agree?
It is believed that people nowadays don't do enough exercise. What do you think?
Why do you think so many people these days would like to become famous?
Some people think that going on holiday abroad is better than in your country. Do you agree?
People say that we are becoming too dependent on technology. What's your opinion?
It is thought that  students should spend more time learning practical things. Do you agree?

Speaking Part 4 B

Use Vocaroo https://vocaroo.com/ to record an audio in which you answer the following questions (3 minutes).

Do you believe that living in a city offers more opportunities for career advancement compared to living in a rural area?
Many argue that social media has a negative impact on mental health. What are your thoughts on this issue?
There's a debate about whether traditional classroom learning is more effective than online education. Where do you stand on this topic?
Some suggest that the government should provide free healthcare only for citizens who work. What is your perspective on this proposal?
It's often said that hobbies and leisure activities are important for maintaining a healthy work-life balance. Do you agree or disagree with this statement?
There's a growing concern about the environmental impact of fast fashion. What actions do you think individuals and companies should take to address this issue?
